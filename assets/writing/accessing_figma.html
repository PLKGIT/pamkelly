Accessing Figma

Overview

You can login to Figma from a web browser or the Figma Desktop App.

In this article, we will cover:

  1. Logging into Figma from a Web Browser

  2. Logging into Figma with the Desktop App

Web Browser Login

From a Web Browser

Navigate to www.figma.com in your web browser.

Click the Log in link at the top right side of the browser window.

Enter your login credentials or authenticate with your Google Account or SAML SSO credentials.

Click the Log in button.

Figma opens to the Recently Viewed page after log in.

Click your name at the top left side of the browser window to open the account options dropdown. Select Account Settings to modify your account or Log Out to log out of Figma.

TOP

Desktop App Login

From the Figma Desktop App

Double-click the Figma app in your Applications folder.

Enter your login credentials or authenticate with your Google Account or SAML SSO credentials.

Click the Log in button.

The Desktop app opens to the Recently Viewed screen after log in.

Click your name at the top left side of the screen to open the account options dropdown. Select Account Settings to modify your account or Log Out to log out of Figma.
